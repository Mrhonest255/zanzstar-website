Safari Blue Tour

The ultimate Zanzibar marine adventure - sailing, snorkeling, and seafood feast

Full Day (8 Hours) 2-20 People 4.9 (150+ Reviews)

Tour Overview

Safari Blue is Zanzibar's most iconic marine excursion and a must-do experience for every visitor. This full-day adventure takes you on a journey through the stunning Menai Bay Conservation Area aboard a traditional wooden dhow boat.

Your day begins with a scenic sail across crystal-clear turquoise waters, passing pristine sandbanks and mangrove islands. Along the way, you'll have multiple opportunities to snorkel above vibrant coral reefs teeming with tropical fish, sea turtles, and colorful marine life.

The highlight of the trip is a sumptuous seafood feast served on a picturesque sandbank that appears at low tide. Enjoy freshly grilled fish, lobster (seasonal), calamari, prawns, and an array of tropical fruits while surrounded by nothing but ocean and sky.

After lunch, swim in the warm Indian Ocean waters, relax on the sandbank, or explore the mysterious mangrove lagoons by swimming through natural channels. The return journey offers spectacular views as the sun begins its descent toward the horizon.

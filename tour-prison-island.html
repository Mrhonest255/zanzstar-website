Prison Island Tour

Meet giant tortoises and explore this historic island sanctuary

Half Day (4 Hours) 2-20 People 4.7 (180+ Reviews)

Tour Overview

Prison Island, also known as Changuu Island, is one of Zanzibar's most popular excursions and a perfect half-day adventure. Located just 20 minutes by boat from Stone Town, this small island offers a unique combination of wildlife encounters, historical exploration, and beach relaxation.

The island's main attraction is its sanctuary of giant Aldabra tortoises, some of which are over 100 years old! These gentle giants were originally brought from the Seychelles and now roam freely in a protected sanctuary. You'll have the opportunity to get up close, feed them, and take memorable photos.

Beyond the tortoises, explore the historic ruins of the prison that was built in 1893 but never actually used for prisoners. Instead, it became a quarantine station for yellow fever cases. Today, the atmospheric ruins provide a fascinating glimpse into the island's past.

The island also boasts beautiful beaches with crystal-clear turquoise waters, perfect for swimming and snorkeling. Colorful fish and coral reefs await just off the shore.

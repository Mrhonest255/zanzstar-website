Stone Town Walking Tour

Discover the rich history and culture of Zanzibar's UNESCO World Heritage site

3-4 Hours 1-15 People 4.8 (200+ Reviews)

Tour Overview

Step back in time and explore the enchanting streets of Stone Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the cultural heart of Zanzibar. This walking tour takes you through narrow winding alleys, past ancient Arab houses with ornately carved wooden doors, bustling bazaars, and significant historical landmarks.

Your expert local guide will share the fascinating stories of sultans, slaves, and traders that shaped this unique city. From the somber history of the slave trade to the colorful tales of spice merchants and royal intrigue, Stone Town's past comes alive with every step.

Visit iconic sites including the Old Fort, the House of Wonders, the Anglican Cathedral built on the former slave market, and the childhood home of legendary rock star Freddie Mercury. End your tour at the vibrant Darajani Market where locals shop for fresh produce, spices, and seafood.

Tour Itinerary

  • 9:00 AM - Meeting Point

    Meet your guide at Forodhani Gardens

  • 9:15 AM - Old Fort (Ngome Kongwe)

    Explore the 17th-century Omani fort

  • 9:45 AM - House of Wonders

    Visit the former Sultan's palace and museum

  • 10:30 AM - Anglican Cathedral & Slave Market

    Learn about Zanzibar's slave trade history

  • 11:15 AM - Freddie Mercury House

    See where the Queen frontman was born

  • 11:45 AM - Traditional Doors Walk

    Discover the famous carved Zanzibar doors

  • 12:15 PM - Darajani Market

    Experience the local market atmosphere

  • 1:00 PM - Tour Ends

    End at Forodhani Gardens or your hotel

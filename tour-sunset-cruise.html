Sunset Dhow Cruise

Sail into the golden sunset on a traditional wooden dhow

2-3 Hours 2-30 People 4.9 (300+ Reviews)

Tour Overview

Experience the magic of Zanzibar as the sun dips below the Indian Ocean horizon on our enchanting Sunset Dhow Cruise. This romantic and relaxing excursion is perfect for couples, honeymooners, or anyone seeking a peaceful end to their day in paradise.

Board a beautifully decorated traditional wooden dhow, the same type of vessel that has sailed these waters for centuries. As the white sails catch the evening breeze, you'll glide across the calm waters while the sky transforms into a canvas of orange, pink, and purple hues.

Sip on refreshing drinks and enjoy light snacks as you watch the Stone Town skyline fade into silhouette against the spectacular sunset. The gentle motion of the boat, the sound of lapping waves, and the stunning natural beauty create an unforgettable atmosphere.

This is the perfect way to celebrate a special occasion, create romantic memories, or simply unwind after a day of exploring. Many guests describe this as one of the highlights of their Zanzibar trip.

What to Expect

  • 4:30 PM - Check-in & Welcome

    Arrive at the departure point near Forodhani Gardens

  • 5:00 PM - Set Sail

    Board the traditional dhow and begin your cruise

  • 5:15 PM - Refreshments Served

    Enjoy drinks and light snacks as you sail

  • 6:00 PM - Sunset Views

    Watch the spectacular sunset over the Indian Ocean

  • 6:30 PM - Photo Opportunities

    Capture the magical colors of twilight

  • 7:00 PM - Return to Shore

    Sail back as the stars begin to appear

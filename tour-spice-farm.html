Spice Farm Tour

Discover why Zanzibar is called the Spice Island

3-4 Hours 2-15 People 4.9 (250+ Reviews)

Tour Overview

Zanzibar has been known as the "Spice Island" for centuries, and this aromatic tour is your chance to discover why. Visit a traditional spice farm where exotic plants have been cultivated for generations, and experience the sights, smells, and tastes that made Zanzibar the center of the global spice trade.

Your knowledgeable guide will lead you through lush gardens filled with cloves, vanilla, cardamom, cinnamon, nutmeg, black pepper, and many more exotic spices and tropical fruits. Learn how each spice is grown, harvested, and used in cooking, medicine, and beauty treatments.

This hands-on experience allows you to touch, smell, and taste fresh spices straight from the source. You'll learn to identify plants you may only know in dried form, and discover the traditional uses of each spice in Zanzibari culture.

The tour includes a traditional Swahili lunch featuring dishes prepared with fresh spices from the farm, giving you a true taste of local cuisine.

Spices You'll Discover

  • Cloves

    Zanzibar's most famous export, used in cooking and medicine

  • Vanilla

    Fresh vanilla pods with their intoxicating aroma

  • Cinnamon

    Harvested from tree bark, a sweet warming spice

  • Cardamom

    Known as the "Queen of Spices"

  • Nutmeg & Mace

    Two spices from one fruit

  • Black Pepper

    The "King of Spices"

  • Lemongrass

    Fragrant herb used in cooking and tea

  • Tropical Fruits

    Jackfruit, durian, breadfruit, starfruit, and more
